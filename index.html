About Us

Welcome to Rising Rock Co.! I’m a crystal lover and artist passionate about sharing the magic of nature’s raw energy through vibrant crystals, hand-carved specimens, and one-of-a-kind painted rock art.

Every piece is intentionally chosen — whether natural, dyed, or crafted — to spark beauty, resonance, and a touch of cosmic curiosity. From ethereal carvings to original painted creations, each item is handpicked for its energy, visual power, and unique story.

Our live shows are laid-back and honest, featuring low starting bids, fast shipping, and surprises like giveaways, grab bags, and rare finds you won’t see twice. Join us for good vibes and glowing gems!
